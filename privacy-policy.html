Privacy Policy

Gmail Thread Button  ·  Opatow LLC  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Gmail Thread Button ("the extension") is published by Opatow LLC. It adds a single toggle button to Gmail that switches your inbox between threaded and unthreaded conversation view. This policy describes the limited technical data the extension accesses and how it is used.

Data we do not collect

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, email content, account credentials, or browsing history. No data is ever sent to any server operated by Opatow LLC or any third party.

Local storage

The extension stores a single value in your browser's local storage — the detected tonal side of Gmail's current theme (dark or light). This value is used only to prevent a brief white flash when Gmail reloads after saving settings. It never leaves your device.

Screen pixel sampling

To detect whether Gmail is using a dark or light theme, the extension captures a screenshot of the active Gmail tab using Chrome's chrome.tabs.captureVisibleTab API and samples a small number of pixels from the Gmail toolbar area. The screenshot is processed entirely in memory within the extension's service worker; no image data or pixel values are stored to disk or transmitted anywhere. The screenshot is discarded immediately after the colour values are read.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

activeTab — required to capture a screenshot of the Gmail tab for theme detection as described above.

storage — required to persist the detected theme tone across Gmail page reloads, as described under "Local storage" above.

Access is limited to https://mail.google.com/*. The extension does not run on any other website.

Third parties

The extension does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking code.

Children's privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
